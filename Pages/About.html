Makani Project




The Makani project, the Makani Centers program, or the Child Friendly and Safe Spaces are multiple names for the Safe Spaces project, supported by UNICEF, launched in many Arab and African countries with the aim of providing safe places and spaces for children and youth to access learning opportunities, child protection services and other important services. It provides play areas, psychological treatment, and safe education for all children and members of these centers with experts in communicating with children who have suffered from displacement and social violence.

Education Project



Since education is a fundamental right of every child, the Ministry of Education and its partners in the education sector (UNICEF, UNHCR, UNESCO) are making collective efforts to improve access to inclusive quality education for all children and refugee children, and to meet the urgent needs for access to appropriate education.
